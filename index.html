About Me

I will code for bacon and biscuits. I like to eat at the table with humans as I don't bite. I demand that my employer trim and even polish my nails so that I always look professional. No matter how adorable I appear, I'm never getting another hair cut! Baths will be subject to my human's approval, since it depends on her taste buds and soap since she got a mouth full during her last try. I require two (2) fifteen minute walks and one (1) sixty minute nap during my work day. Rub my belly and I'm your friend for life ... unless you have a cat. Oh, I can be quite clumsy and have been known to fall from my chair while typing or get tangled in computer cords and accessories when running around to alleviate my frequent boredom. Well, enough about me as all you really need to know is that I'm lovable and most importantly, AVAILABLE. Yes, you're going to hire me. Thank you for listening, looking forward to working with you, and please think about giving me a diamond collar for my excellent ninety (90) day performance review.
